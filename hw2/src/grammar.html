******************Grammar*******************
expr → simple expression ((relationship operators) simple expression)?
********************************************
*/
expr ::= simpleExpression ( <RELATIONAL_OPERATORS> simpleExpression )?

******************Grammar*******************
simple expression → (+|-)? term (+|-|or term)* | term
********************************************
*/
